Kirtan

Kirtan is a musical form of meditation call and response chanting. The chants are in the Ancient language of sanskrit , some of these chants have been uttered or sung for thousands of years. The sacred chants bring deep healing, open our hearts, and bring deep peace and stillness within.

Anna

Anna began her musical career in her late teens. Singing with the band "The Presence". She moved to Canada at the age of 23 and sang with various bands performing at clubs and festivals. At the age of 29 she attended a yoga retreat in the Bahamas where she was introduced to kirtan. She fell in love with it and upon returning to Canada was guided to her spiritual teacher Guru Mangala Amshumati.
